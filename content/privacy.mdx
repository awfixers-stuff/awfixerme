This Privacy Policy describes how **AWFixer** (“we”, “us”) handles information when you visit **AWFixer’s Lounge** and related pages on this site (the “Site”). It is meant to be clear and practical; it is not legal advice.

**Last updated:** April 14, 2026

## Information we collect

- **Information you provide** — for example, when you use the [Contact](/contact) form or message us, we receive whatever you choose to send (such as your name, email address, and the content of your message).
- **Account and authentication data** — if you sign in, our authentication provider **Clerk** processes sign-in and account-related data according to their service and your chosen sign-in method (for example email or a social provider). Clerk’s privacy practices are described in their documentation and policies.
- **Technical and usage data** — like many sites, we may collect technical information sent by your browser (such as IP address, general location derived from IP, device and browser type, and timestamps). We may use **Vercel Analytics** to understand aggregate traffic and performance (typically page views and related metrics, not individualized marketing profiles).

We do not sell your personal information.

## Cookies and similar technologies

We and our service providers may use cookies, local storage, or similar technologies that are required for the Site to function (for example to keep you signed in or remember preferences) and to measure usage. You can control cookies through your browser settings; disabling some cookies may limit certain features.

## How we use information

We use the information above to:

- operate, secure, and improve the Site;
- respond to your messages and requests;
- understand aggregate usage and reliability; and
- comply with applicable law and protect our rights and users.

## Service providers

We rely on trusted vendors to host and operate the Site (for example **Vercel** for hosting and analytics, and **Clerk** for authentication). Those providers process data under their own terms and privacy policies, only as needed to provide their services to us.

## Data retention

We keep information only as long as needed for the purposes above, unless a longer period is required by law. Retention periods can vary depending on the type of data and the systems involved.

## Your choices and rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict certain personal data, or to object to some processing. To make a request, contact us using the [Contact](/contact) page. We will respond in line with applicable law.

## Children’s privacy

The Site is not directed at children under 13 (or the age required in your region). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

## International visitors

If you access the Site from outside the country where the services are hosted, your information may be transferred to and processed in other countries where privacy laws may differ.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the “Last updated” date above. Continued use of the Site after changes means you accept the updated policy.
