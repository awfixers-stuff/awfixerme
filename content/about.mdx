AWFixer is the public home for projects, experiments, and writing. The Lounge is the shell: glass panels, soft gradients, and navigation that stays out of the way.

## What you’ll find here

- **Build notes** — how things ship, not just that they shipped.
- **Opinions** — tools, platforms, and craft; always up for a better idea.
- **Contact** — reach out from the [Contact](/contact) page or wherever you already follow along.

## Colophon

This site runs on [Next.js](https://nextjs.org), [Tailwind CSS](https://tailwindcss.com), and content in the repo so it versions with the code. Thanks for visiting.
