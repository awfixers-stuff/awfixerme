Dark backgrounds reduce glare for evening sessions. The crimson accent carries identity without shouting.

### Practical tips

1. Keep body copy at comfortable line length.
2. Use muted text for secondary detail.
3. Let one accent color do the heavy lifting.

That balance is what makes the Lounge feel like a room, not a billboard.
